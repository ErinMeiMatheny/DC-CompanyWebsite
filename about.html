About Us

Here at the Happy Possums Cafe & Eatery, we believe all animals should be treated equally. While Possums are generally viewed as pests ,they are actually loving and helpful creatures. Not only are they vegan friendly (herbivores),but they also can be beneficial in waste management.Our products are organic, and ecofriendly, with recyclble paper cups and recycled dining ware.A portion of our proceeds go toward environmentalist foundations.The Happy Possum Cafe & Eatery is a place for everyone, that is judgement free!
